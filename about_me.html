Trust in the process

My theme towards life (and in return, my portfolio) is to trust in the process. The
same way that flowers somehow manage to bloom in the spring after the harshness of the
winter, and how the sun warms your body up after months of cold darkness. Life has a
wicked sense of humour; so sit back and enjoy the show sometimes: things will find their way.

My point is: create a beautiful life. Embark on all its adventures. Create memories.
Create a long-lasting effect, so that other people can, too, see flowers within the snow.

Through my projects, I have seen first-hand what *creating* can do to the world. Whether
it's solving healthcare challenges, fostering diversity in my work, or designing something
beautiful to make someone smile, I create to make a difference.
